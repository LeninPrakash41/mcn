# MCN Industry Adoption Strategy

## Vision Statement
Make MCN the de facto standard for AI-powered business automation scripting by 2026.

## Target Markets

### Primary Markets
1. **Enterprise Automation** - Fortune 500 companies
2. **AI/ML Startups** - Rapid prototyping and deployment
3. **Business Process Automation** - RPA and workflow tools
4. **Cloud Platforms** - Native integration with AWS, Azure, GCP

### Secondary Markets
1. **Educational Institutions** - Computer science curricula
2. **Consulting Firms** - Digital transformation projects
3. **Government Agencies** - Public sector automation
4. **Healthcare** - Medical data processing and automation

## Competitive Positioning

### vs Python
- **Advantage**: AI-native, business-focused
- **Message**: "Python for AI automation"

### vs JavaScript/Node.js
- **Advantage**: Simpler syntax, built-in AI
- **Message**: "JavaScript simplicity with AI power"

### vs PowerShell
- **Advantage**: Cross-platform, modern syntax
- **Message**: "PowerShell for the cloud era"

### vs Bash/Shell
- **Advantage**: Structured programming, error handling
- **Message**: "Shell scripting evolved"
